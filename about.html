About Me

Anthony is a recent graduate of the Mechanical Engineering & Management program at McMaster University, a joint 5-year program with Degroote School of Business with an engineering specialization in Mechanics and Design, and business specialization in finance. In 2014, he studied abroad as an exchange student at Nanyang Technological University in Singapore for 10 months taking a courses in the School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, Nanyang Business School, and School of Art, Design and Media, while exploring the exciting cultures across South East Asia. His experience abroad inspired him to pursue a career with global outreach and impact in the fields of finance or technology.

Anthony is currently actively seeking a business/finanial analyst or related role in financial services or technology company/start-up. Feel free to reach out about new opportunities!
